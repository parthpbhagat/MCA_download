Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does the ZIP files contain?

    The ZIP file contains the PDFs for each company, organized in folders as per the categories filed on the MCA website. To ensure that each file name is unique, it is prefixed with the document ID.

  • Can I get documents of LLP, private and Public companies as well?

    Yes, you can get access to all the documents of any Indian Company being it is LLP, OPC, private or public. There is no need for a company to be listed to get its financials as long as the company has submitted its financials to MCA.

  • How much time it takes to download one company?

    The time required to download documents for a company cannot be predefined, as it depends on several factors, including:

    • 1. Number of Files to Download: The more documents filed by the company, the longer it may take to download all of them.
    • 2. Size of the Files: Larger files will naturally take more time to download compared to smaller ones.
    • 3. Availability of MCA Servers: The responsiveness and availability of the MCA servers can significantly impact the download speed.
    • 4. Internet Speed: A faster internet connection will reduce the download time, while a slower connection will increase it.
    • 5. Under optimal conditions, timeframe for downloading documents for a company typically can vary based on the factors mentioned above.
  • How long will the company be available on my dashboard?

    The company ordered by you can be download till the expiry of 7 Days and also you will have the option to update at No Cost in 7 Days, if there are new filings.

  • Can I ask for Update in Download?

    You can udpate your downloaded list of files upto 7 Days from the date of order. It will be udpated if there are any files has uploaded by the company.

  • Do you store my MCA username and password?

    We never ask for your MCA Userid and Passwords.

  • What happens if the MCA website is down?

    We are the facilitator to download the public records available on the MCA website, we are not a reseller of public records and only ensure quick and convenient download of files. In case of connectivity issues, we try our best to download the documents ASAP.
